# Privacy Policy — Tower Stack

_Last updated: Fri, 29th May, 2026_

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game **Tower Stack** ("the App") handles information when you use it.

## We Do Not Collect Personal Information

The App does not ask for, or collect, personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

## Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data only on your device:

- Your best score
- Your sound, music, and vibration preferences

This data never leaves your device. We have no access to it. Uninstalling the App removes it.

## Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements served by **Google AdMob**, a service provided by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and process information about you in accordance with Google's own privacy practices, including:

- A non-personally-identifiable advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID on Android, IDFA on iOS)
- Device information (model, OS version, language, country)
- Approximate location derived from IP address
- App usage data related to ads (impressions, clicks)

This information is used by Google to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance.

For full details, see Google's privacy policy: <https://policies.google.com/privacy>

You can reset or limit ad tracking through your device's system settings:

- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID
- **iOS**: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us using the address below and we will take appropriate action.

If the App appears in Google Play's **Designed for Families** program, ad serving is limited to family-appropriate content through AdMob's family-safe content filtering.

## Permissions

The App requests the following permissions:

- **Internet access** — to load advertisements
- **Network state** — to check connectivity before requesting ads
- **Vibration** — for haptic feedback on perfect stacks (can be disabled in Settings)

## Data Retention

Local preferences and your best score remain on your device until you uninstall the App or clear app data. No data is retained on any external server because no data is collected.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the most recent revision.

## Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to: **developer@playoasis.net**
